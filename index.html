The Waffle Fest

Waffle sticks, pops, sundaes, sandwiches, waffle cakes, red velvet waffles and savoury waffles. Bet it, you never tried Butter Chicken waffles or Old monk waffles, right? Combine it with a plethora of other desserts like pies, tarts, cakes and brownies. Already drooling?

Just delhiing presents THE WAFFLE FEST at Select CITYWALK, a three day fest dedicated to your sweet tooth. Want more reasons to attend this amazing three day fest? They have one! Live performances will be conducted to add on the chill.

So get ready for the sweetest fest in town because it's gonna be wafflelicious! For enquiries, call on 8447383004 or write on justdelhiing@yahoo.in

 Date: 3 rd , 4 th ,5 th november,2018

 Time: 10:00 a.m.

 Venue: Select CITYWALK, New Delhi A-3 District Centre New Delhi India 110017

 Nearest Metro Station: Malviya Nagar, yellow line

 Entry: free entry, only pay for what you eat.

Horn OK Please!!

Courtesy : eattreat.in

If you also plan on getting HIGH on food this winter (like us!) then we’ve just the right place for you. ‘The Horn Ok Please Food Truck Festival’ is one of north India’s largest and most loved food festivals with treats ranging from mouth-watering waffles to beautifully detailed Freak Shakes and Monster Shakes! And for all those who are planning on staying away from food to get rid of the so-called ‘Holiday-weight’, scent of sizzling BBQs and kebabs will surely change your minds.

 Date : 16th – 18th November, 2018

 Time : 12:00 PM onwards

 Venue : Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium Marg, New Delhi 110003

 Stay tuned to the So Delhi Facebook page for more information about ticket sales!

Dilli Tere Ishq Mein

For the Love of Delhi and it’s glorious culture and pride.

This art and cultural exhibition showcases Delhi with an objective to throw light on the city’s rich, colourful history and the culture that emerged out of it. This festival is the event to experience all of ‘Dilli’- Food, music, art and much more that will make you fall in love with the city and its heritage.

 Where- Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium, New Delhi

 When- 23-25 November, 2018

 Timings- 12pm to 10pm.

By: Apoorva Singhal, cse 2, 3 rd year, Btech

Life In Color Music Festival

World’s largest paint party- here in India for the first time all the way from Miami!

Life in Color is a music festival which is going to be a gigantic dance party with bulks of paint drenching the audience throughout. There will also be DIY paint stations where you can make art on walls, and many more exciting and colourful activities. Get your dance moves ready for grooving to the world’s biggest DJs, stunning performances, live art and the life in color paint blast experience!

 Where- Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium, New Delhi

 When- Sunday, 18 th November,2018

 Time-3pm onwards

 GA- 1500/-

 VIP- 2500/-
